The Fallout Series

Throughout the gameplay of the multiple Fallout games the player is able to encounter many fascinating scenarios and work to solve them for the betterment of the world around them. The games are open world and allow the player to explore into side quests and venture beyond what is initially laid out for the storyline of the game. Along the way the player encounters missions that initially seem to be nothing more than a task, but instead actually present a profound statement about a social or political stance.
